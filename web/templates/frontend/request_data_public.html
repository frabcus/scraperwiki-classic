If you've found a cool dataset that you don't know how to liberate, post it on our community Google Group. Someone might be able to give you some tips or even write the scraper for you.

If you make your request exciting and straightforward, you might get more of a response. Here are some suggestions of things to include.

  • Title
  • Why you want to liberate this data
  • URLs or source documents
  • Walkthrough of how you would get the data if you needed to resort to copy-and-paste, which could include
    • Column names of the resulting tables
    • Screenshots
  • How often the site gets updated (if you know)

If your request sounds boring or overly commercial, people probably won't respond; please consider our premium services if that is the case.
