Isn’t it really frustrating when you’ve got data but it’s locked away in PDFs or split up across thousands of different web pages?

ScraperWiki specialises in extracting and structuring that data, so you can find new leads or gain business insights.

We deliver the data in exactly the format you need, every time its updated, so you’re always one step ahead of the competition.

How do you get my data?

Our data consulting page has case studies and details about:

  • The kinds of data we can extract
  • How we clean your data
  • Integration with your systems
  • Visualisations and applications

I can’t afford to pay!

If you're a non-profit or don't have a budget, post your data request on our community email list. Make sure to mention:

  • Why you want to liberate this data
  • URLs or source documents
  • How often the site gets updated

Remember it's all voluntary. If your request sounds boring or overly commercial, people probably won’t respond. Use the form to the left instead.
